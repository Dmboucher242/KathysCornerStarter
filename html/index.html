WHAT WE DO

We have joined to start a grassroots gardening company to bring you a variety of flowers and plants so you can continue your love for gardening, or for those starting out to find your passion for gardening. Almost all the plants you will find on our web page from flowers, to vegetable plants, to herbs, and even bushes and trees have been collected from local gardens or sourced from local seed companies. In our collection you will find a variety of all types of plants that are designed to flourish in Colorado's climate and our shorter growing season here in the Rocky Mountains.

HOW WE DO IT

Here at Kathy's Corner we encourage you to spread the love of gardening with your family and friends. We encourage you to start seed harvesting and seed sharing with those communities around you that need some plants in their life. If you need help with any questions on gardening, seed harvesting, or seed starting, we are here to help answer anything we can for you. You can email any questions to us here at KathysCornerNursery@gmail.com

WHY WE DO IT

Our love of gardening sprouted from time spent with family and friends over the last 40 years and we have developed a strong passion for gardening from those that came before us and raised us. Building roots in the soil and our relationships, we have learned to understand and appreciate the beauty, simplicity and complexity of our natural surroundings, and invite you to join us in spreading flowers and plants as well as the love of gardening to your family and loved ones.
